Presenting to you a smart educational tool from Team PANDOR of IMS Engineering College. “EDUFREE”.

One of the most controversial issues facing educators today is the topic of educating students with disabilities. With the advent of modernization, the trend and the technology have changed, but the way of thinking of people still remains the same. Society often neglects the children and the people that have some kind of learning disabilities. Due to this, most of the time they receive negative feedback from their schools and could not be able to develop any kind of positive attitude to the outside world.

  • This is an AI based smart tool for helping the students with disabilities to have access to the vast world of online education and to widen up their spectrum of knowledge. Edufree helps them to search their doubts and topics using the smart voice bot which cannot just recognize their voice commands but can also respond in the form of audiobooks which can make the study efficient and easy for those special kids.
